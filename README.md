## Rainfall Impact on Agricultural Productivity

## 📊 Project Overview
Power BI Dashboard analyzing the relationship between monsoon rainfall and crop yields in India (1966-2017)

## 🔍 Key Features
- Rainfall trend analysis
- State-wise production comparison
- Correlation between rainfall and production
- Seasonal rainfall analysis
- Geographic visualization

## 📁 Files Included
- Pankaj_Khurana_CPDA_B10.pbix

## 💡 Insights
- **High Regional & Annual Variability**: Rainfall levels fluctuate significantly by both year and region.
- **Positive Correlation:** A moderate positive relationship exists between rainfall volume and agricultural production.
- **Top-Performing Region:** West Bengal leads with the highest production and rainfall level.
- **Seasonal Peak:** Rainfall is heavily concentrated, peaking during the monsoon months like July & August.
- **Resource Efficiency:** Certain states achieve superior agricultural efficiency with lower or optimal water utilization like Bihar & Orissa.
